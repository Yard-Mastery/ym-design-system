# 01 · Brand Foundation

Who Yard Mastery is, who we talk to, and how we sound. **Load this for every piece of content** — voice is the through-line that makes everything feel like us.

---

## 1. What we are

Yard Mastery sells **pro-grade lawn and yard care products built for DIY homeowners**. We take the formulas, knowledge, and routines that professional lawn care operators use and package them so a regular homeowner can get the same results in their own yard — without paying for a recurring service.

We're a product company *and* an education company. We sell fertilizers, bio-stimulants, grass seed, soil tests, pre-emergents, and tools — but we win because we teach people *how* and *when* to use them (via email, the Yard Mastery App, blogs, and YouTube).

**Founding DNA:** Yard Mastery grew out of the work of **Allyn Hane, "The Lawn Care Nut" (LCN)** and a community of DIYers figuring out what actually works on real lawns. That community + expert credibility is core to the brand.

## 2. Positioning

> **For** DIY homeowners who take their lawn seriously, **Yard Mastery is** the lawn care brand that delivers professional-grade results, **because** our products use the same formulas the pros use and we guide you on exactly what to apply, how much, and when.

**One-liners we live by:**
- "Build the Yard of Your Dreams." *(primary brand line)*
- "Pro-grade lawn care products for DIY homeowners." *(descriptor)*
- "DIY Lawn Care, Done Right." *(editorial)*
- "The same results the pros get — without the recurring service bill."

## 3. Who we talk to (audience)

Primary: **the DIY lawn enthusiast** — usually a homeowner (skews male, 30–65, suburban U.S.) who mows his own lawn, watches lawn YouTube, and wants the best-looking yard on the block. Ranges from curious beginner to obsessive "lawn nut."

**The single most important segmentation we use: warm-season vs. cool-season lawns.** Almost every seasonal email is split this way. Always know which one you're addressing.

| | Warm-season | Cool-season |
|---|---|---|
| Grasses | Bermuda, Zoysia, St. Augustine, Centipede, Buffalo | Kentucky Bluegrass, Tall Fescue, Perennial Rye, Fine Fescue |
| Region | South / Sun Belt | North / Transition zone |
| Peak feeding | Late spring–summer | Spring & fall |
| Seeding | Spring/summer (or plugs/sod) | **Fall** (the big seeding push) |

Secondary audiences: new homeowners starting their first lawn project; gardeners (vegetables/flowers via the organics line); gift buyers (Father's Day, holidays).

## 4. Brand pillars (what we always prove)

1. **Pro Formulas** — "the same formulas used by lawn care professionals, not watered-down big-box mixes." Lead with potency and real ingredients (e.g., Bio-Nite™, iron, humic, kelp).
2. **DIY Friendly** — clear application guides, coverage math done for you, the App, and a community of DIYers. "We meet you where you are."
3. **Real Results** — thousands of DIYers transforming their lawns season after season. Show the green.
4. **Expert Support** — step-by-step guidance from the Yard Mastery team + LCN's expertise. "Spray like the pros."

Supporting proof points to reach for: **Made in America**, **no-burn** formulations, **2-year shelf life**, **free shipping on most orders**, and the **Yard Mastery App** (calendar, soil-test-driven plans, AI Lawn Helper).

## 5. Voice & tone

**Voice (always):** A confident, encouraging coach who happens to be a lawn expert. Plain-spoken and direct. Talks straight to **"you."** Genuinely excited about lawns and about *your* success. Knows the science but never lectures.

Think: the knowledgeable buddy at the fence line who's three steps ahead of you and wants you to win — equal parts pep talk and pro tip.

**Tone (dial up/down by context):**
- *Educational email / blog:* patient, clear, step-by-step. More teacher than salesman.
- *Promo / sale email:* punchy, urgent, benefit-first, lots of energy and caps.
- *Seasonal reminder:* timely and practical — "here's what your lawn needs right now."
- *Community / brand:* warm, proud, a little folksy ("Lawns are where memories are made").

### Voice principles
- **Benefit before feature.** "Thicker, greener lawn — fast" *then* "24-0-6 with 3% iron."
- **Second person, active, imperative.** "Feed your lawn now." "Beat the summer heat."
- **Confident, not hedgy.** We know this works; say so.
- **Make it easy.** Do the math for them (coverage, timing, what-to-buy).
- **Earn urgency honestly.** Tie urgency to the *season* and the *lawn*, not just the sale ("Apply before the pre-emergent window closes").
- **A little swagger is on-brand.** "Dominate." "Go dark." "Spray like the pros." Keep it confident, never arrogant or mean.

### Do / Don't (voice)
| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| "Get a thicker, greener lawn — fast." | "Our product utilizes advanced nitrogen technology." |
| "Here's exactly what to put down this month." | Vague hype with no action ("It's amazing!"). |
| "Same formula the pros use." | Knock customers for not knowing — never condescend. |
| "Your lawn is hungry. Feed it." | Over-promise / fake guarantees. |
| Talk to one person ("you," "your yard"). | Corporate "we are pleased to offer our valued customers." |

## 6. Messaging by season (always-on calendar)

Use this to pick the right message for the time of year. (See `04-products.md` for the exact products.)

- **Winter (Dec–Jan):** Holiday/Christmas-lights line (Q4 seasonal product), gift cards & merch, planning for next season, soil testing, "new year, new lawn."
- **Early Spring (Feb–Mar):** **Pre-emergent timing is everything** (crabgrass prevention — Prodiamine), soil test, spring green-up, starter fert for new lawns.
- **Late Spring (Apr–May):** Feeding season ramps (Flagship), warm/cool season value packs, **Memorial Day sale** (biggest spring promo).
- **Summer (Jun–Aug):** Heat & drought survival — Stress Blend, Moisture Max, Spoon Juice; Father's Day (Backpack Sprayer as a gift); "give yourself more time."
- **Fall (Sep–Nov):** **Cool-season seeding push**, fall pre-emergent (Dimension/dithiopyr), Double Dark for color, Pre-Ban winterizer, naturals; **Halloween "Domination" pack**, Black Friday/Fall clearance ("2-year shelf life — stock up").

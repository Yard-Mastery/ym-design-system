## 1. Naming conventions (follow exactly)

- **Lead with the NPK number, then the name:** "**24-0-6 Flagship**," "**7-0-20 Stress Blend**," "**0-0-7 Prodiamine**." (NPK = the Nitrogen-Phosphorus-Potassium ratio on the bag.)
- **Trademark on the proprietary ingredient:** **Bio-Nite™** (first prominent mention). Yard Mastery granular fertilizers are "with Iron & Bio-Nite™."
- **Coverage shorthand we use everywhere:** "18 lb covers 6,000 sq ft · 45 lb covers 15,000 sq ft" (varies by product — see tables).
- **Standard claims to reach for:** "No burn," "Safe for all grass types," "2-year shelf life," "Made in America."
- **Brand house:** Yard Mastery's own line is the hero. We also carry **N-Ext / Greene County Fertilizer** (liquids), **Jonathan Green**, **GCI** & **Double Dark** seed, **Earthway** spreaders, **MySoil**, and **Celsius WG**. Keep YM front and center.

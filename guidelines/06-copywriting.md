# 06 · Copywriting

Formulas, vocabulary, and swipe copy for writing like Yard Mastery. Built from real campaign language in the email archive. Pair with `01-brand-foundation.md` (voice).

---

## 1. The copy formula
**Benefit → proof → action.** Lead with what the reader gets ("thicker, greener lawn — fast"), back it with a quick proof (NPK, iron %, Bio-Nite™, "pro formula"), then tell them exactly what to do ("Shop now," "Apply before the window closes").

Default structure for a promo:
1. **Headline** — the promise or the offer, short & bold.
2. **Subhead** — the benefit in one believable sentence.
3. **Body** — 1–3 short lines: what it is, who it's for, why now.
4. **CTA** — one clear action verb.

- "Natural Lawn Fertilizers That Do More for Less"

- "Made in America, for American Lawns"
- "Stop Fall Weeds Now!"
- "Time for Fall Pre-Emergents"
- "Go Dark Now" / "Get a Spooky Dark Green Lawn"

**Headline formulas:**
- *Outcome + speed:* "Thicker, Greener Lawn — Fast."
- *Beat the [season problem]:* "Beat the Summer Heat & Drought."
- *Do [action] now:* "Feed Your Lawn Now." / "Stop Crabgrass Before It Starts."
- *Season + product:* "Summer Starts with Stress Blend."
- *Emotional/lifestyle:* "The Best Memories Start in the Yard."

Keep headlines 3–7 words, **bold UPPERCASE** in graphics. Active and confident.

## 3. Calls to action (approved verbs)
Primary: **SHOP NOW · BUY HERE · SAVE HERE · SAVE NOW · SHOP ALL · LEARN MORE · WATCH HERE.**
Contextual: **DOWNLOAD NOW** (App/e-book) · **CHECK YOUR CALENDAR** (App) · **SHOP THE [SEASON] COLLECTION** · **VIEW HERE** · **GET YOURS**.
- One primary CTA per section; repeat it down a long email.
- Buttons: Roboto Bold, UPPERCASE, on **Action Green `#02AE65`**.

## 5. Offer & sale language (formulas)
We run frequent, energetic promos. Patterns that recur:
- **% off by category list ("SALE INCLUDES"):** e.g., "Natural Nutrients 40% OFF · Bio-Stimulants 20% OFF · Soil Test 30% OFF · YM Fertilizers 15% OFF · Packs 20% OFF · Build-A-Boxes 15% OFF · Grass Seed 20% OFF · & MORE." Highly scannable, very on-brand.
- **Bundle savings:** "Save $$$ when you bundle." / "Buy 3 Quarts, Get the 4th FREE." / "Save up to 25% on packs."
- **Price + strikethrough:** "45 lb $59.99 (was $79.99)."
- **Coupon codes:** short, ALLCAPS, themed — e.g., `FLPREBAN`. Format: "Use code: `CODE`."
- **Threshold/free shipping:** "Free shipping on most orders."
- **Named sales:** Memorial Day, Fall Sale, Halloween "Domination" Pack, Black Friday/Cyber, Father's Day, Spring Kickoff.

### Urgency lines (use honestly)
"Only a Few More Hours to Save!" · "Hurry! Sale Ends Tonight!" · "Time Is Ticking — Stock Up" · "Ends Tomorrow" · "While Supplies Last." Tie urgency to season when possible ("before the pre-emergent window closes," "before the summer blackout starts June 1").

## 6. Email subject lines
Style: short, benefit/urgency-forward, occasional emoji or "!", sometimes first-person from Allyn/LCN.
- "Beat the Summer Now" · "Don't Let the Heat Stress Your Yard"
- "Backpack Sprayers Back in Stock & On Sale"
- "Only a Few More Hours to Save!" · "Hurry! Sale Ends Tonight!"
- "I'm No Longer Carrying [X] — 40% Off Remaining Stock" (first-person, candid)
- "Time for Fall Pre-Emergents" · "Christmas Lights Are In!"

Pattern ideas: *Benefit + season* · *Urgency + deadline* · *News/restock* · *First-person from the founder*. Add a **preview/pre-header** that complements (doesn't repeat) the subject.

## 7. Approved vocabulary & phrases
**Use:** pro-grade · pro formula · DIY / DIYer · thick, green, lush · feed your lawn · green-up · no burn · safe for all grass types · warm-season / cool-season · the pros · dial in · stock up · dominate · go dark · root zone · soil health · 2-year shelf life · Made in America · Bio-Nite™ · your calendar / your next step.

**Use carefully / avoid:** medical or absolute guarantees ("guaranteed perfect lawn"); disparaging customers; over-technical jargon without payoff; competitor names; "cheap" (say "value" / "save"). Don't overuse exclamation points in educational content (fine in promos, in moderation).

## 8. Microcopy & sign-offs
- App nudge: "Your next lawn step is waiting in your calendar."
- Soil test: "Know your soil. Grow better."
- Community/sign-off (LCN voice): warm, first-person, "Keep it green," "See you in the yard," "Let's grow."
- Footer essentials: physical address (Bradenton, FL) + **Unsubscribe**.

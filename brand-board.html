Imagery — do & don't

Do
  • Real, achievable results — thick, even green turf
  • Product in context (on the lawn, in hand, in use)
  • Warm natural light; vivid-but-believable greens
  • Clean space for headline + logo
Don't
  • Fake neon / plastic-looking grass
  • Cluttered or gloomy/cold-blue scenes
  • Competitor or big-box products
  • Let AI render the logo/text — composite real assets

Product naming

24-0-6 Flagship · 7-0-20 Stress Blend · 16-0-0 Double Dark · 0-0-7 Prodiamine

Always lead with the NPK number, then the name. Trademark Bio-Nite™. Coverage: 18 lb → 6,000 sq ft · 45 lb → 15,000 sq ft. Full catalog in guidelines/04-products.md.

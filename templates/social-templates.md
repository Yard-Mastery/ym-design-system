# Social Templates

Fill-in skeletons for organic and promo social. Replace `[BRACKETS]`. Sizes in `05-layout-and-channels.md`; image prompts in `prompts/`; voice in `06-copywriting.md`. Keep captions tight, value-first, one clear CTA. Hashtags: a small, relevant set (see bottom).

---

## 1. Instagram / Facebook feed — Product feature (4:5 or 1:1)
- **Visual:** product-on-lawn or action shot (`prompts` §2/§3). Reserve top third for headline.
- **On-image headline:** **`[BENEFIT, UPPERCASE]`**
- **Caption:**
  > `[Hook — the benefit or problem]`. `[Product w/ NPK]` `[what it does in one line]`. `[Proof: iron %, Bio-Nite™, coverage]`.
  > `[Soft CTA: Link in bio / Shop now]` 🌱
- **CTA:** Link in bio / Shop now

## 2. Tip / Education carousel (1080×1350 slides)
- **Slide 1 (cover):** **`[NUMBER] [SEASON] LAWN TIPS`** + bold turf image
- **Slides 2–N:** one tip each — short headline + 1–2 lines. Tie 1 tip to a product naturally.
- **Final slide:** recap + **CTA** ("Save this. Get the products — link in bio.") + logo
- **Caption:** `[Expand on the topic, coach voice]` + `[CTA]` + hashtags

## 3. Before / After (carousel or split)
- **Visual:** slide 1 "before" (thin/pale), slide 2 "after" (thick/dark) — or composited split with divider.
- **Caption:** "`[Timeframe]` and `[product/routine]`. Here's the plan: `[1-2-3 steps]`." + CTA

## 4. Sale / Promo (1:1 or 4:5)
- **Visual:** branded color-block background (`prompts` §8) + product cutout.
- **On-image:** **`[SALE NAME]` — `[X]% OFF`** + **`[CODE]`** + small CTA chip
- **Caption:** "`[Sale]` is on — `[what's included]`. Ends `[date]`. `[CTA]`." + hashtags

## 5. Story / Reel cover (9:16)
- Keep text/CTA in the **middle 80%**. Big bold headline, one product, "Swipe up / Tap to shop."
- Reel idea hooks: "Stop doing `[mistake]`," "Do THIS before `[season]`," "Watch this lawn go from `[X]` to `[Y]`."

## 6. YouTube thumbnail (1280×720)
- Background from `prompts` §9. **3–5 word** bold high-contrast text added in layout. Expressive face optional. Never clutter.
